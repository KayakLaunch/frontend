About this route

Country United Kingdom
Paddling Environment Coastal
Distance to Paddle 20km
Route Type One Way
Paddling Time
  • Kayak: 4.5 - 5 hours
  • Canoe: 6 - 6.5 hours
  • SUP: 6 - 6.5 hours

Hazards on this route

Weather
  • Significant waves and wind can be experienced, as the area is highly exposed to the Atlantic Ocean to the west
  • Water temperatures are often low. Dry suits should be considered for most of the year
Environment
  • Few landing sites are available along the coast; this is a committed journey
  • Numerous submerged and semi-submerged rocks are present along the cliffs, along with swell and tidal currents
    • These rocks are excellent for rock hopping, however a helmet should be worn in these areas
Traffic
  • Little to no traffic is present close to the coast
  • Larger ships may be seen away from the coast
Wildlife
  • Seals occupy some of the beaches near Godrevy Point. Paddlers should not land on these beaches, nor disturb the seals
  • Dolphins and Basking Sharks are found in the area at times. Paddlers should avoid disturbing them
