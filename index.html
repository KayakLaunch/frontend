What's KayakLaunch?

Planning a kayak trip requires knowledge of the terrain; where to park, where to launch, etc. KayakLaunch does the scouting for you, helping you plan your kayak trips with ease


Launching Spots

Finding suitable places to launch or land a kayak can be challenging. KayakLaunch does the heavy lifting, showing you the launching spots used by local kayakers

Food

When kayaking in unfamiliar places, finding a good spot for lunch can be a matter of trial and error. KayakLaunch removes the errors by showing you where local kayakers go the chow down


Parking

No-one wants to carry heavy kayaks far to reach a launching spot. KayakLaunch shows you the nearest parking spots, along with how much they cost and what forms of payment they accept.

Bathrooms

After hours on the water, there’s often one common question; where to… go! KayakLaunch helps you find the nearest public toilets quickly, along with information on how much they cost to use

Kayak School

For kayak instruction, I highly recommend Hightide Kayak School in Interlaken
