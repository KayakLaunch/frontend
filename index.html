What is KayakLaunch?

Whether you paddle a kayak, canoe, or SUP, KayakLaunch helps you plan your next trip by providing a free to use map launching spots and facilities, and details of paddling routes used by other paddlers.

Launching Spots

Our map of launching spots provides you with information about:

  • Where you can launch or land your kayak, canoe, or SUP
  • Parking near launching spots, including how much they cost
  • Tips for local restaurants and food shops near launching spots
  • And… where the nearest public toilets can be found!

Paddling Routes

Our listing of paddling routes helps you plan your next trip with:

  • Information on routes others have already paddled
  • Tips for where to stop for lunch
  • Information about known hazards along the route
  • Downloadable maps of the route to follow
