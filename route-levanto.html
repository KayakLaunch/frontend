Levanto to Vernazza

Half a day of rock hopping along the picturesque Italian coast from the fishing village of Levanto to Vernazza, one of the Cinque Terre and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

About this route

Paddling Environment Coastal
Distance to Paddle 14.5km
Paddling Time
  • Kayak: 4 - 4.5 hours
  • Canoe: 4 - 4.5 hours
  • SUP: 3 - 3.5 hours

Hazards on this route

Weather
  • High temperatures and UV levels are common in the summer
  • High winds can be experienced in poor weather
Environment
  • Numerous submerged and semi-submerged rocks are present along the cliffs, along with swell and tidal currents
    • These rocks are excellent for rock hopping, however a helmet should be worn in these areas
  • Waves and swell coming landward from the sea can form in certain weather conditions
Traffic
  • Private and fishing boats are common in the harbours at Levanto, Monterosso al Mare, and Vernazza
  • Ferries dock at the pier on the eastern beach in Monterosso al Mare
Wildlife
  • A Marine Protected Area covers much of the coastal waters around the Cinque Terre. Special care must be taken to avoid disturbing wildlife or disrupting the sea bed here.
