Where did KayakLaunch come from?

As a regular sea and touring kayaker, some years ago I started keeping a map of all the places I'd been kayaking. I wanted an easy reference to help me plan my own paddling trips, which had more detailed information than was offered by other databases of launching spots available online.

After getting very board in Barcelona airport one day, and in need of a pet project, I decided to build a website to share this map with other paddlers, and KayakLaunch was born!
