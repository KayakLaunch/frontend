Bönigen to Iseltwlad

A relaxed paddle along Lake Brienz's southern shore to the lake side village of Iseltwald. Ideal as a day trip with a stop for a leisurely for lunch.

About this route

Country Switzerland
Paddling Environment Lake
Distance to Paddle 14km
Paddling Time
  • Kayak: 2.5 - 3 hours
  • Canoe: 2.5 - 3 hours
  • SUP: 2 - 2.5 hours

Hazards on this route

Weather
  • Significant changes in weather can occur during the day, due to the mountainous terrain
  • Small waves can form in stronger winds
Environment
  • Submerged rocks are present in the shallow waters, where the cliffs give way to rocky shores
  • Cliffs make short portions of the route “committed”, meaning there’s no quick exit from the water
Traffic
  • Ferries dock at the piers in both Bönigen and Iseltwlad regularly. Local laws require paddle craft stay 50m away from them at all time
  • Private boats and white-water rafts often use the boat ramps in Bönigen during the summer months
  • Private boats enter and exit the harbour in Bönigen regularly
Wildlife No wildlife hazards known
