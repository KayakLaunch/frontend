Paddling Routes

Bönigen to Iseltwlad

A relaxed paddle along Lake Brienz's southern shore to the lake side village of Iseltwald. Ideal as a day trip with a stop for a leisurely for lunch.

Lachen Round Trip

An active day's paddling through the southern end of Lake Zürich, through the canals Pfäffikon, and in between the islands of Ufenau and Lützelau.
